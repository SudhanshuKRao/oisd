# OISD.FUN

### Summary Notes for OISD Documents

OISD.FUN is created to share concise and easy-to-understand summary notes for OISD (Oil Industry Safety Directorate) documents. The purpose of this website is to help students and professionals prepare for interviews and technical discussions.

---

## Purpose

* To provide clear and structured summary notes.
* To support interview preparation and learning.
* No original OISD documents or confidential content are shared. Only summaries are provided.

---

## Disclaimer

This website does **not** share or host official OISD documents.
All content is written in summary form for educational purposes only.
